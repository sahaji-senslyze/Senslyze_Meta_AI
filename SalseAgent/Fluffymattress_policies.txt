8. Product Care Guidelines
8.1 Mattress Care
* Rotation: Rotate the mattress 180 degrees every 3-6 months to ensure even wear.
* Protection: Use a mattress protector to prevent stains, spills, and allergens.
* Cleaning:
o Spot Cleaning: Clean spills immediately with a mild detergent and a damp cloth. Do not soak the mattress.
o Deep Cleaning: Professional cleaning recommended annually.
* Avoid Direct Sunlight: Protect the mattress from prolonged exposure to direct sunlight to prevent material degradation.
8.2 Pillow Care
* Fluffing: Fluff pillows daily to maintain their shape and support.
* Washing:
o Removable Covers: Machine washable covers can be removed and washed according to the care label.
o Non-Removable Covers: Spot clean with a mild detergent and a damp cloth.
* Drying: Air dry pillows thoroughly to prevent mold and mildew. Some pillows may be tumble dried on low heat as per care instructions.
* Replacement: Replace pillows every 1-2 years for optimal support and hygiene.
8.3 General Tips
* Avoid Sharp Objects: Prevent punctures and tears by keeping sharp objects away from mattresses and pillows.
* Weight Distribution: Avoid placing excessive weight on one area to maintain product integrity.
* Storage: If storing, keep products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture.
